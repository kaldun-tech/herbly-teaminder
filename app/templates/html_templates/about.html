About Herbly TeaMinder

Your Personal Tea Steeping Assistant

Herbly TeaMinder is designed to help tea enthusiasts achieve the perfect cup every time. Whether you're a casual tea drinker or a connoisseur, Teaminder helps you track and time your tea steeping with precision.

Features

  • Precise steeping timers for each tea
  • Temperature recommendations
  • Steep count tracking
  • Personal tea collection management

Why Track Steeping?

Different teas require different steeping times and temperatures to achieve their optimal flavor. Over-steeping can lead to bitterness, while under-steeping might not release the full flavor profile. By tracking these parameters, you can consistently brew the perfect cup of tea.

Quick Tea Guide

Tea Type Temperature Time
Black or Dark 200-212°F 3-5 min
Green 170-180°F 2-3 min
Oolong 180-190°F 2-5 min
White 160-170°F 1-3 min
Herbal 212°F 5-7 min
